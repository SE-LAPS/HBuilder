# Image Guide for HBuilder App

## Required Images

### Banner Images (8 images)
Place these images in `assets/images/` folder:

1. **banner1.jpg** - Car wash service banner (1920x600px recommended)
2. **banner2.jpg** - Auto detailing banner
3. **banner3.jpg** - Premium wash service banner
4. **banner4.jpg** - Membership offer banner
5. **banner5.jpg** - Express wash banner
6. **banner6.jpg** - Eco-friendly wash banner
7. **banner7.jpg** - VIP service banner
8. **banner8.jpg** - Special promotion banner

### Service Center Images (8 images)
Place these images in `assets/images/` folder:

1. **center1.jpg** - Downtown Premium Wash (800x600px recommended)
2. **center2.jpg** - Uptown Auto Spa
3. **center3.jpg** - Brooklyn Car Care
4. **center4.jpg** - Queens Express Wash

6. **center6.jpg** - Staten Island Shine
7. **center7.jpg** - Midtown Express Clean
8. **center8.jpg** - Harlem Wash & Shine

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Banner dimensions**: 1920x600px (or 16:5 ratio)
- **Service center dimensions**: 800x600px (or 4:3 ratio)
- **Max file size**: 500KB per image for optimal performance

## Where to Get Images

You can get free car wash images from:
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/s/photos/car-wash)
- [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/search/car%20wash/)
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/images/search/car%20wash/)

## Quick Setup

1. Download 16 images total (8 banners + 8 service centers)
2. Rename them according to the list above
3. Place them in `H:\HBuilder\assets\images\` folder
4. Run `flutter pub get`
5. Run your app!

## Temporary Placeholder

If you don't have images yet, the app will show a car wash icon as a placeholder.
